About Me

Hi!
My name is Shaun.

I'm originally from Durban, South Africa, now living in Dublin and I'm a self-taught Web Developer on a never ending quest to master this art of Front-End development. I'm passionate about all kinds of technology on the hardware and software side and I'm always looking for the next big thing out there to pique my interest.

I'm confident in my ability to create clean and responsive mobile first web sites. Currently I have learnt and know languages such as HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript along with the Bootstrap framwork and libraries such as React and JQuery.
I'm open to any project you may have in mind, so if you need a site, give me a shout.

Now that you know a little bit about me, why not head over to my Portfolio or if you want to get in touch with me, check out the Contact section at the bottom of this page.

Thanks for visiting.
